PuntGuide was built by a group of Australian punters who've spent years using and testing every major betting platform in the country.

We've bet on everything from Group 1 racing to NBA, MLB, NFL, NRL, AFL and more, and we know firsthand what separates a good bookmaker from a frustrating one. From clunky apps and slow withdrawals to clean interfaces and fast payouts, we've seen it all.

Between us, we've tested every major betting app in Australia extensively, and we understand what actually matters when you're betting. Not just odds, but usability, reliability, and how the platform performs when it counts.

That's why PuntGuide exists. To cut through the noise and help you choose the right bookmaker based on how you actually bet, not just promotions. How we review bookmakers

Every bookmaker review on PuntGuide is assessed against the same set of criteria. We open and use accounts at each bookmaker we review, and we update our assessments when products change.

🏆
Odds quality
How competitive the fixed odds are across AFL, NRL, racing and other markets compared to rival bookmakers.
📋
Market range
Depth of betting markets - head-to-head, line, totals, player props, same-game multis and exotic options.
💰
Bonuses & promotions
Welcome offers and ongoing promotions assessed on their actual value - wagering requirements, expiry and restrictions.
📱
App & platform
Mobile app quality on iOS and Android - speed, design, functionality and reliability during peak periods.
⚡
Payout speed
How quickly withdrawals are processed and how straightforward the verification process is.
🛡️
Licensing & trust
ACMA licensing status, responsible gambling tools, account limit policies and customer support quality.

Editorial independence

PuntGuide maintains strict separation between our commercial relationships and our editorial content. Bookmakers do not pay to appear on PuntGuide or to receive a higher rating.

Our rankings are determined entirely by the criteria above. A bookmaker that pays us a higher affiliate commission does not receive a higher rating as a result. If a bookmaker's product worsens, we update our rating accordingly - regardless of any commercial relationship.

For a full explanation of how we rank sites and how affiliate relationships work, see our Editorial Policy.
