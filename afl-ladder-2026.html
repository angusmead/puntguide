🔥 Story of the Ladder
Fremantle's rise to the top of the AFL ladder is the story of 2026. At 7-1, the Dockers have the strongest percentage in the top 4 and look every bit a genuine premiership contender. Sydney at 3rd have the competition's best percentage (178.1%) — a sign of a team dominating opponents rather than merely winning. The surprise is Melbourne in 4th, recovering strongly from last season's disappointments.
⚔️ The Percentage Battle
Six teams sit on 16 ladder points from 7th to 11th — Gold Coast, Geelong, Adelaide, Western Bulldogs, North Melbourne and Brisbane. Percentage will be decisive for finals positions. Sydney's extraordinary 178.1% already gives them insurance against any poor results in upcoming rounds.
📉 Bottom 4 Watch
Richmond are yet to win a game in 2026 — their percentage of 54.2 is the worst in the competition. Carlton and Essendon both sit at 1-6. West Coast's 55.8% is alarming for a club that needs priority draft picks. This group are almost certainly playing for draft position from here.
