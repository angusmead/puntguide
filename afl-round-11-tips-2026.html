Sir Doug Nicholls Round 2026

The AFL honours First Nations peoples across all nine games this round. All teams wear specially designed First Nations guernseys. The round is named for Sir Doug Nicholls, a Yorta Yorta man, AFL pioneer and former Governor of South Australia.
