Short Answer

Seven games across the first bye round. The injury news dominates — Collingwood lose Elliott (ACL, season over), Moore (hamstring) and Hayes (shoulder), while Essendon are catastrophically short-handed without McGrath, Redman, Durham, Guelfi and Caldwell (Archer May cleared to play). Sydney also hit with four confirmed changes — Mills and Papley both out with calf injuries, Rosas ankle, Grundy managed — with debutant Harry Kyle set to be unveiled. Value plays: St Kilda +18.5 against a Hawks side projected to win by 13, Carlton +24.5 with Carroll's ACL tightening the Cats’ margin, Brisbane −5.5 at the Gabba with Berry and Answerth back (Zorko out six weeks with quad), and West Coast −7.5 against a five-man-down Essendon.
