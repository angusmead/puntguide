⭐ Match of Round - Melbourne -30.5 $1.90
Analysis
Melbourne are at home at the MCG against an Essendon side priced at $5.10 — that gap reflects how dominant the Demons have been in 2026. Max Gawn anchors the ruck and Jack Steele powers a midfield that also leans on Ed Langdon’s run. Bayley Fritsch takes his opportunities up forward, Kysaiah Pickett provides pace and unpredictability, and Jake Melksham returns to add another option with Lukas Cooke and Joel Fitzgerald in for Brody Mihocek (inj) and Caleb Windsor (inj).
