Our Methodology

How we rate Australian betting sites

We assess every bookmaker across five criteria, weighted by importance to a typical Australian punter.

Odds competitiveness (30%). We record head-to-head and line prices on AFL, NRL and racing across hundreds of markets each month and compare against the field. One bookmaker consistently offering 3% worse prices than rivals is a real-money difference over a year of betting.

Market depth (25%). How many markets do they offer beyond head-to-head? Player markets, same-game multis, exotic racing bets, international sports coverage. A bookmaker that only covers the basics scores lower here.

App quality (20%). We test every app on iOS and Android for load speed, live betting responsiveness, bet slip reliability, and crash frequency. A good-looking app that freezes during grand finals is not a good app.

Withdrawal speed (15%). We test with real accounts and time from submission to funds cleared. PayID and bank transfer both tested. Times are averages across multiple tests.

Account treatment (10%). How quickly does the bookmaker restrict or limit winning accounts? We track reports from our community and weight this in our rankings. No bookmaker scores well on this — but some are significantly worse than others.

📋 No paid placements: Every ranking on this page is editorial. Bookmakers cannot pay to be listed higher. We earn commission when you click through and open an account — but this is the same regardless of where a bookmaker ranks. Our incentive is accuracy, not promotion.
