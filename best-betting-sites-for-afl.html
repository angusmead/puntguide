Tips for AFL Bettors

How to bet on AFL smarter

Line betting beats head-to-head for value. The line market forces bookmakers to set a spread, which often creates pricing inefficiencies — especially in mismatched games where casual money pushes the favourite price down.

Player disposal markets reward research. Bookmakers set disposal lines using season averages and recent form — but they often don’t fully account for matchup-specific factors like opposition midfield strength or whether a player is playing through injury.

SGMs are high-margin products — use them selectively. The bookmaker’s margin compounds across every leg you add to a same-game multi. They are entertaining but not where serious value is found.
