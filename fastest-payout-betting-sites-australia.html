Important Context

What affects withdrawal speed

PayID is always faster than bank transfer. If you want same-day access to winnings, set up PayID with your bookmaker. Every major bookmaker now supports it.

Verification delays are a one-time thing. First withdrawals from a new account often take longer while identity verification completes. Once verified, times normalise to what we tested above.

Weekend and public holiday timing matters. Our tests were on a Tuesday. Withdrawals submitted Friday afternoon may not process until Monday morning regardless of the bookmaker.

Avoid ApolloTech-platform bookmakers for fast payouts. Several smaller bookmakers run on the ApolloTech platform — withdrawal times on this software are consistently slower than BetMakers or BetCloud-based platforms.
