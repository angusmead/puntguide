How We Assess Horse Racing Tips

Our racing analysis goes beyond the form guide. Here's the framework we use for every selection:

📋
Form Analysis
Recent run times, sectional data, winning margins, race class and opposition quality. Form in similar conditions to today's race is weighted most heavily.
🏟
Track & Distance
Horses perform differently at different distances and tracks. Course-and-distance winners are given significant weight. Track bias on heavy or soft tracks is crucial.
🌧
Going & Weather
Track conditions (firm, good, soft, heavy) affect performance dramatically. Horses with wet-track form ratings have a significant edge when rain is forecast.
🎯
Barrier Draw
The barrier draw matters most in small fields and on tracks with known track biases. Inside barriers at venues like Randwick are historically advantageous.
💰
Fixed vs Tote
We always compare fixed odds with Best Tote before recommending. On longer-priced horses, the tote regularly pays better than fixed. BetRight Best Tote is the best product for this.
🐴
Trainer & Jockey
Strike rates at specific tracks, class levels and distances. A top-percentile trainer-jockey combination in peak form adds genuine confidence to any selection.
