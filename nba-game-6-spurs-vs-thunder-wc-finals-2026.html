Analysis

Wembanyama needs to impose. The towering Frenchman shot 4-of-15 in Game 5 — a clunker by his standards — and seemed reluctant to attack the paint. At home in a must-win, expect a more physical Wemby. He’ll draw fouls, control the glass, and set the tone defensively. The bounce-back game is real for elite players in playoff must-wins.

OKC’s injury report is a genuine factor. Mitchell is out entirely. Williams hasn’t played and there’s no indication he’s close. SGA will have to shoulder a heavier load, and San Antonio knows it. Expect the Spurs to make life very hard for Gilgeous-Alexander all night.

Home court in a closeout game. The Spurs are −3.5 favourites at Frost Bank Center and the crowd will be electric. Historically, teams in Game 6 elimination scenarios at home perform above expected. The $1.60 reflects that — but given OKC’s injury state, it could be even shorter.
