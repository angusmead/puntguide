Series Context

The Eastern Conference Finals pit the Knicks - well-rested after sweeping Philadelphia - against a Cavaliers side running on empty. Cleveland has not had consecutive days off since April 29 (Game 5 vs Toronto Raptors). They then went seven games against top-seeded Detroit, finishing that series with just one day separating them from Game 1 here.

The Knicks last played on May 10. Nine days off. Brunson is healthy. KAT is healthy. OG Anunoby is probable with a hamstring issue but is expected to play. This is a significantly more rested side in their own building in front of what will be one of the loudest crowds of the year.

The previous three regular season meetings between these teams produced totals of 228.5, 231.5 and 241.5. The playoff total of 217.5 reflects tighter defence but perhaps underestimates just how much Brunson and company have been in attack mode all postseason. When the Knicks get rest, they come out scoring - 137 points in Game 1 against Philly after just three days off.
