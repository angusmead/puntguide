How the NFL Super Bowl Works

The NFL regular season runs 18 weeks across September to January. The top 7 teams from each conference (AFC and NFC) qualify for the playoffs — 14 teams in total. The playoffs consist of three rounds: Wild Card, Divisional and Conference Championship, before the Super Bowl.

The Super Bowl is played in early February between the AFC champion and the NFC champion. It is a single elimination final — there is no best-of-series format. Super Bowl LXI is scheduled for February 2027.

Key betting consideration: The NFL's single-game elimination playoff format means any team that reaches the Super Bowl has a genuine chance of winning on the day — even heavy underdogs. This makes NFL futures betting higher variance than other sports, and explains why outright value can often be found in the $15–$25 range rather than backing the favourite.
