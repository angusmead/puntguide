How We Make Our NFL Tips

NFL betting is different from Australian sports — the spread (line) is the primary market and value assessment requires understanding both teams and the specific betting number. Here's our approach:

📊
Power Rankings
Weekly assessment of all 32 teams based on offensive/defensive DVOA, recent results and injury-adjusted performance.
📉
Line Analysis
The point spread is the key NFL market. We assess whether the line accurately reflects the true gap between teams.
🏥
Injury Reports
NFL injury reports are published Wednesday–Friday. Late changes to key players — especially QB — can shift lines significantly.
🌤
Weather & Venue
Outdoor cold-weather games affect passing offences. Dome teams playing outdoors in winter face a significant disadvantage.
🔄
Schedule Spots
Teams on short rest (Thursday games), off a bye, or playing in a different time zone all have measurable performance impacts.
💰
Market Comparison
