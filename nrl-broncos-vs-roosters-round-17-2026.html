Match Preview

Friday night under the Suncorp lights and the Broncos welcome back Origin trio Reece Walsh, Kotoni Staggs and Payne Haas. The bad news is much worse than the good: Adam Reynolds (hamstring) and Ezra Mam (shoulder) are both OUT. Ben Hunt slots in at halfback, Xavier Willison shifts to lock to accommodate Haas, and Blake Mozer is the new face on the bench.

Losing both your primary playmakers in the same week is fatal at Suncorp, where the Broncos have been outright bad in 2026 (2-5 home record). Without Reynolds' goal-kicking accuracy and Mam's attacking creativity, Brisbane have to rely on Ben Hunt - at 35 and a long way removed from his halfback prime - to steer them around a fully-fit Roosters spine.

The Roosters get Hugo Savala back from concussion into the centres, with Billy Smith out to the wing and Tommy Talau to the interchange. Daly Cherry-Evans at six and Sam Walker at seven is one of the best half pairings in the competition right now. The Roosters are 4th on the ladder with a 4-3 away record and they've been the more organised side all season. Roosters $1.59 H2H is the call. Mark Nawaqanitawase is the form winger - aerial threat against a Broncos right edge that won't have Mam's coverage.
