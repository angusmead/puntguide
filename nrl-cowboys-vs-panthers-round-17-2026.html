Match Preview

Townsville on a Saturday twilight and the reigning premiers are coming off a rare loss - they will be furious. Nathan Cleary, Isaah Yeo and Brian To'o all return after being rested in Round 16. Paul Alamoti pushes back to the centre, with Luke Garner, Jack Cogger and Liam Henry sliding to the interchange. Casey McLean (quad) and Mitch Kenny (leg) remain sidelined.

The Cowboys named just one change - Liam Sutton added to the six-man bench at the expense of John Bateman. The headline: Jason Taumalolo has been named to start his 300th NRL match alongside Thomas Mikaele in the front row. The Cowboys are 9th on the ladder but have lost 4 of their last 5 - momentum is in the wrong direction.

This is a Penrith "response" game and the Panthers are devastating when stung. With Cleary, Yeo and To'o back together for the first time in three weeks, the spine is at full strength. The Cowboys' Jaxon Purdue-Jake Clifford half pairing has been competitive at home but is a level below Cleary-Talagi. The line is huge at -13.5 but Penrith's pattern after losses is to win big. Panthers $1.23 H2H is the banker of the round. Brian To'o is the tryscorer - fresh from Origin and back among the points.
