Match Preview

Thursday night curtain-raiser at CommBank Stadium and the storyline writes itself. Mitchell Moses returns from Blues duty for the 250th game of his NRL career, with Ronald Volkman switching to five-eighth to accommodate. The Eels are 14th on the ladder having only mustered five wins from 14 games and despite their captain's return, this is a side that has leaked points all season.

The Rabbitohs get their own captain back - Cam Murray returns from State of Origin duty after missing the emotion-charged Round 15 win over the Broncos. Lachlan Hubner drops back to the bench. Souths have hit form at the right time, gone 2-1 in their last three including that Brisbane win, and the Latrell-Cody Walker-Johnston spine has clicked into gear.

Souths' away form (2-4) is the concern but Murray's return adds the leadership and workrate that has been missing on Origin weekends. Moses returning is great theatre but he's one man - the Eels' issues across the rest of the spine and pack remain. Rabbitohs $1.53 H2H is the call. The line at -6.5 is fair but the H2H is the cleaner play. Alex Johnston is the anytime tryscorer - the NRL all-time try record holder rarely takes a week off.
