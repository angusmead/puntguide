Match Preview

The Knights will be without their two Origin representatives in Kalyn Ponga and Bradman Best. Good news: Dylan Lucas is available for selection after being left out by Laurie Daley, so he returns to the second row in a boost. As for how Newcastle will cover for the Origin omissions, Fletcher Sharpe goes to fullback as expected with Fletcher Hunt joining the starting side in the centres. Sandon Smith partners Dylan Brown in the halves to cover for Sharpe's move to fullback.

The Dolphins are also without a host of Origin stars, but the biggest name missing is halfback Isaiya Katoa, who is set for an extended stint on the sideline with a fractured wrist. Brad Schneider fills in for Katoa as expected, which will put more pressure on Kodi Nikorima to step up in his halves partner's absence given Schneider is more limited as a playmaker. Given Nikorima is without a contract for next year, it gives him an opportunity to rise his price tag.

Elsewhere, Trai Fuller is called upon to replace Hamiso Tabuai-Fidow at fullback while he is on Origin duty. Tevita Naufahu and Felise Kaufusi also return to the starting side in place of Queensland representatives Selwyn Cobbo and Tom Flegler. There is good news on the injury front with Tom Gilbert (throat) back while Jake Averillo (hand) also returns in a welcome boost.

The market has this as a coin flip with a 1.5-point line. Both sides depleted but the Dolphins have more experience in their replacements - Fuller has been the alternate fullback all year, Kaufusi captains from prop, Schneider is a competent NRL halfback. Newcastle's Sharpe (a natural five-eighth) at fullback and rookie Sandon Smith at six are downgrades. Our tip: Dolphins $1.84 H2H. Jamayne Isaako anytime tryscorer $1.75.
