Match Preview

Sunday afternoon at McDonald Jones Stadium and the Knights get a triple boost. Kalyn Ponga and Dylan Lucas are back from being rested, and Jermaine McEwen returns from a club-imposed suspension. Fletcher Sharpe reverts to five-eighth, with Sandon Smith and Thomas Cant going to the bench. NSW Cup front-rower Lachlan Crouch is in line for an NRL debut off the bench.

The Tigers welcome back Adam Doueihi from a Round 9 shoulder injury at halfback, replacing Jock Madden (concussion). Lock Alex Twal is back from a knee injury, with Alex Seyfarth shifting to the bench. The bad news: Patrick Herbert is OUT with a shoulder, so Starford To'a pushes to centre and Jeral Skelton comes onto the wing. Latu Fainu is recalled on the bench.

The Knights are 5th on the ladder with three straight wins. The Ponga-Dylan Brown-Sharpe spine with Bradman Best returning to centres is a level above the Tigers' Doueihi-Luai pairing. McDonald Jones has been a strong home venue (5-2), and the Knights' middle with Saifiti, Mooney and McEwen is a forward pack the Tigers can't match. Knights $1.42 H2H. Greg Marzhew at left winger is the tryscorer - reliable finisher at home and a regular in the Knights' attack.
