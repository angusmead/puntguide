Match Preview

The Panthers have lost two games in a row - shock defeats to the Titans and Cowboys - something unheard of this season. Now Penrith front up missing four players from last week's loss. Nathan Cleary, Isaah Yeo and Liam Martin are on Origin duty; Isaiah Papali'i is out after suffering a head knock against the Cowboys. In a boost, Brian To'o returns at No.5 after being axed by the Blues.

Jack Cogger starts at 7 in place of Cleary - Cogger has been decent in a pinch-hitting role off the bench, coming in relief for Cleary plenty of times before, so Ivan Cleary will have every confidence he can help turn Penrith's form around. Lindsay Smith at lock in place of Yeo; the new-look back row is Liam Henry and Scott Sorensen.

The Rabbitohs are trending up. Jack Wighton back from a two-month arm injury - his timing perfect given Cam Murray (Origin) and the injured Latrell Mitchell both miss. Wighton lines up at centre with Siegwalt the other centre. Wighton is nearing the end of his career but has plenty to offer in attack and defence, and gives Souths a major near-the-line threat. Tallis Duncan - sensational in the centres this year - shifts to the back row to accommodate Wighton. Expect Souths to look for Wighton early and often. Debutant Dayne Jennings makes his first NRL start on a flank, replacing Alex Johnston (calf, a couple of weeks). Matt Dufty on the six-man bench chasing his first NRL game since Round 12. Lachlan Hubner starts in the middle for Murray.

If Penrith were at full strength this would be a tough game to tip. As it is, the Rabbitohs won't win easily but you'd have to think they will win. Our tip: Rabbitohs by 8. At $2.26 the value is with the visitor. Cody Walker at $3.30 for anytime tryscorer.
