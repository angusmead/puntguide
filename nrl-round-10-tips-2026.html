Round 10 Storylines

Taumalolo's record (Cowboys v Eels): Jason Taumalolo becomes the Cowboys' most-capped player in game 295, passing Johnathan Thurston. A massive milestone for one of the great NRL forwards.

Ashton Ward named at halfback (Rabbitohs v Sharks): Wayne Bennett drops Humphreys and hands the halfback jersey to Ward — one of the boldest selections of the season. Souths also start Jye Gray over the in-form Matt Dufty at fullback.

Zane Harrison's NRL debut (Roosters v Titans): Josh Hannay makes the call to hand the highly touted Harrison his NRL debut at halfback against the competition's hottest team. Brave but risky.

Broncos without Reynolds (Sea Eagles v Broncos): Adam Reynolds is out with a head knock. Tom Duffy steps up at halfback. Combined with the losses of Staggs and Mariner, Brisbane face a tough ask at 4 Pines Park.
