Sharks - Blayke Brailey named in extended squad (broken arm comeback from Round 13). Nicho Hynes (calf) on reserves. Origin stars Fonua-Blake and Nikora in the starting side.

Gold Coast Titans v Penrith Panthers
Saturday 20 June · 5:30 PM · Cbus Super Stadium
⭐ Panthers -12.5 $1.91
Analysis
Twilight on the Gold Coast and the Panthers travel north listing Nathan Cleary, Isaah Yeo and Brian To'o all on the reserves bench to be monitored post-Origin 2. Jack Cogger steps into halfback if Cleary is rested, Lindsay Smith slides to lock should Yeo fail to come up. Casey McLean (quad) is sidelined so Luke Garner and Izack Tago are the new centres with Paul Alamoti out to the wing. Even with all that disruption, Penrith bring a four-man Origin-replacement bench (Billy Phillips, Luron Patea, Jack Cole, Sione Fonua) that most clubs would put in their starting 13. Titans have Tino Fa'asuamaleaui and Jojo Fifita named to back up but Lachlan Ilias' return at #14 doesn't lift the spine enough to keep this close. Panthers at $1.23 H2H — the market expects a comfortable cover even at -12.5.
