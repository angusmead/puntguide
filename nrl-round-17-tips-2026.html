⚡ Casualty Ward & Team News - Round 17
Eels - Mitchell Moses returns from Blues duty for his 250th NRL game. Ronald Volkman switches to five-eighth, Joash Papalii slides to the bench. Ryley Smith and Saxon Pryke listed on the extended bench.
Rabbitohs - Captain Cam Murray is back after missing the emotion-charged Round 15 win over Brisbane on Origin duty. Lachlan Hubner slides to the bench. Dayne Jennings in line for debut after representing NSW U19s last week.
Titans - Co-captain Tino Fa'asuamaleaui returns from a rest. Chris Randall makes his return from concussion at lock for Cooper Bai (head knock). Jensen Taumoepeau season-ended with an ankle injury - Jaylan De Groot comes onto the wing.
Bulldogs - Stephen Crichton at five-eighth again after the move proved a success last week, Matt Burton in the centres. Sitili Tupouniua OUT (hamstring), Josh Curran starts in the back row, Lipoi Hopoi joins the bench. Jacob Preston on reserves chasing a Round 12 arm comeback.
Broncos - Origin trio Reece Walsh, Kotoni Staggs and Payne Haas all return. Adam Reynolds (hamstring) and Ezra Mam (shoulder) miss. Ben Hunt takes over at halfback; Xavier Willison moves to lock to accommodate Haas; Blake Mozer takes the bench spot.
Roosters - Hugo Savala returns from concussion into the centres. Billy Smith slides to the wing, Tommy Talau to the interchange. Toby Rodwell drops out of the 19.
Dolphins - Isaiya Katoa back from a knee injury. Maroons quartet of Tabuai-Fidow, Plath, Flegler and Cobbo all return after a freshen up. Kaufusi, Schneider and Keeley shift to the bench. Trai Fuller and Tavita Naufahu drop to reserves.
Warriors - Jackson Ford OUT (pectoral) - Tanner Stowers-Smith starts at prop. Maroons rep Kurt Capewell returns, Marata Niukore reverts to the bench. Hooker Sam Healey (thumb) makes his return on the interchange.
Cowboys - Just the one change with Liam Sutton added to the six-man bench at the expense of John Bateman. Jason Taumalolo named to start his 300th NRL match alongside Thomas Mikaele after both came off the bench against the Warriors.
Panthers - Nathan Cleary, Isaah Yeo and Brian To'o all return after being rested in Round 16. Paul Alamoti pushes to centre; Garner, Cogger and Henry slide to the interchange. Casey McLean (quad) and Mitch Kenny (leg) remain sidelined.
Sea Eagles - Reuben Garrick sidelined - Josh Feledy starts in the centres. Hooker Brandon Wakeham (calf) and prop Kobe Hetherington miss - Jake Simpkin and Ethan Bullemor join the starting side. Clayton Faulalo on reserves chasing a hamstring comeback.
Storm - The big one: Jahrome Hughes OUT. Tyran Wishart starts at halfback. Harry Grant returns to the starting side; Trent Toelau reverts to the interchange. Josiah Pahulu is the new face on the bench.
Raiders - Simi Sasagi returns from five games out with a shoulder injury. Josh Papalii (calf) remains sidelined. Sasagi's return pushes Seb Kris to the interchange; Chevy Stewart and Jake Clydsdale are the new faces on the bench.
Dragons - Coach Dean Young keeps the same starting 13. Moses Suli (hip) still out. Blake Lawrie and Lyhkan King-Togia make way for Jacob Halangahu and Nick Tsougranis on the extended bench.
Knights - Triple boost - Kalyn Ponga and Dylan Lucas back from rest, and Jermaine McEwen returns from a club-imposed suspension. Fletcher Sharpe reverts to five-eighth, Sandon Smith and Thomas Cant to the bench. NSW Cup front-rower Lachlan Crouch in line for an NRL debut off the bench.
Wests Tigers - Adam Doueihi returns from a Round 9 shoulder injury and takes over at halfback from Jock Madden (concussion). Lock Alex Twal (knee) back, Alex Seyfarth to the bench. Patrick Herbert OUT (shoulder) - Starford To'a moves to centre, Jeral Skelton onto the wing. Latu Fainu recalled on the bench.
Sharks - BYE in Round 17. Return Round 18.

⭐ Rabbitohs $1.53 + Johnston Tryscorer
Analysis

Ronald Volkman switches to five-eighth and Joash Papalii slides to the bench.
