NRL Round 18 Tips, Predictions & Best Bets

Short Answer

A short, Origin-affected Round 18 with just 5 matches and 7 teams on the bye ahead of Origin Game 3 at Suncorp on Wednesday 8 July. Featured games include Eels v Sea Eagles (Moses v the Trbojevics post-Origin), Knights v Dolphins (potential top 4 v top 4 spoiler) and Panthers v Rabbitohs (Friday night opener). Team lists drop Tuesday 30 June afternoon; winner + tryscorer picks for every match will be published immediately after.

PuntGuide Editorial · Round 18 · Fri 3 – Sun 5 July 2026 · Odds: Sportsbet · Tips publish post team lists Tuesday 30 June
⚡ Coming Tuesday - Team Lists & Casualty Ward
Team lists drop Tuesday 30 June 4pm. Heavy Origin rep involvement expected for Game 3 at Suncorp the following Wednesday - Panthers, Knights, Sharks, Eels, Sea Eagles, Broncos, Dolphins, Rabbitohs and Wests Tigers all have Blues or Maroons reps to manage.
Known carryovers from Game 3 squads: Cleary, Yeo, Martin, Haas, Crichton, Best, Murray, Fonua-Blake, Olakau’atu, Brailey, Robson, Moses, Nawaqanitawase, Strange, Koula, Radley, Bostock (NSW debut), Tedesco, Barnett, Young + 20-man Maroons squad listed on our Origin Game 3 preview.
Bye round impact: Bulldogs, Cowboys, Raiders, Roosters, Storm, Titans and Warriors all rest. The 10 playing teams will use this round to either rest stars, blood debutants, or push for ladder position before the finals run home.
Full late changes will replace this notice once team lists are confirmed Tuesday afternoon. Check back for confirmed inclusions, omissions, debutants and injury updates ahead of Friday night kickoff.
