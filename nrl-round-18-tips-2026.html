⚡ Casualty Ward - Round 18 Team News
Panthers - Out: Cleary, Yeo, Martin (NSW Origin) and Isaiah Papali’i (concussion). Brian To’o returns at No.5 after being axed from the NSW Game 3 squad. Jack Cogger at halfback, Talagi at five-eighth, Lindsay Smith at lock. Jack Cole, John Fonua and Luron Patea added to the six-man bench - same crew Ivan Cleary used to rest his Origin stars after Game 2.
Rabbitohs - Out: Cam Murray (NSW Origin) and Alex Johnston (calf, 2-3 weeks). In: Jack Wighton returns at centre after six games out with an arm injury - Tallis Duncan shifts to back row. Last week’s debutant Dayne Jennings takes over on the wing. Hubner moves to lock. Matt Dufty on extended bench chasing his first NRL game since Round 12.
Dragons - No changes to the side that went down narrowly to the Raiders last Sunday. Moses Suli listed in reserves as he chases his return from the hip injury that has cost him the past two matches.
Wests Tigers - Out: skipper Jarome Luai (replaced by Madden) and Taylan May (shoulder). In: Jock Madden returns from concussion at five-eighth; Heamasi Makasini promoted to start at centre; Alex Twal back after four games out (knee) at lock. Seyfarth slides to bench. Charlie Murray joins the six-man bench for Mavrik Geyer.
Broncos - Mass changes. Out: Reece Walsh + Pat Carrigan (QLD Origin), Payne Haas (NSW Origin), Aublix Tawha (ankle) and Josiah Karapani (club suspension). In: Gehamat Shibasaki back from knee injury at centre; Deine Mariner back from thigh injury on the wing; Va’a Semu in at prop for Haas; Perham at fullback. Burleigh Bears forward Luke Gale chasing an NRL debut off the bench alongside Jaiyden Hunt.
Sharks - Out: Addin Fonua-Blake and Blayke Brailey (NSW Origin) and Briton Nikora (QLD Origin). In: Tom Hazelton at prop, Jayden Berrell at hooker, Billy Burns into the back row. New faces on the bench: Tukupa-Ke Hau Tapuha and Harrison Hassett. Nicho Hynes back from calf.
Eels - Out: Mitchell Moses (NSW Origin). In: Jonah Pezet returns from hamstring injury for his first NRL game since Round 5 after a NSW Cup hit-out last week - takes the No.7. That’s the only change to the side that lost to the Rabbitohs in Round 17.
Sea Eagles - Luke Brooks suffered a season-ending ACL injury against the Storm - Joey Walsh takes over at five-eighth. Out: Olakau’atu (NSW Origin) and Tolu Koula (NSW Origin) plus Brooks. In: Clayton Faulalo and Reuben Garrick return from hamstring injuries; Josh Feledy moves to interchange; Corey Waddell comes in for Olakau’atu; Kobe Hetherington back at prop after a game in concussion protocols. Zaidas Muagututia in line for an NRL debut on the extended bench.
Knights - Out: Kalyn Ponga and Bradman Best (both NSW Origin recalls). Fletcher Sharpe slides to fullback to cover Ponga; Fletcher Hunt comes into centres for Best; Sandon Smith moves from bench to five-eighth. Harrison Graham and Francis Manuleleua added to the six-man bench.
Dolphins - Out: Tabuai-Fidow, Plath, Flegler, Cobbo (QLD Origin) and Jack Bostock (NSW Origin debut), plus Isaiya Katoa (fractured wrist). Trai Fuller again at fullback, Tevita Naufahu and Felise Kaufusi join the starting side; Brad Schneider wears the No.7 in Katoa’s absence. Tom Gilbert (throat) back after missing the Warriors game. Jake Averillo returns at centre after hand surgery via QLD Cup. Brian Pouniu and Sebastian Su’a added to bench.
Origin Game 3 absentees confirmed: NSW — Bostock, Crichton, Best, Haas, Robson, Barnett, Young, Tedesco, Nawaqanitawase, Moses, Cleary, Yeo, Martin, Murray, Fonua-Blake, Olakau’atu, Brailey + Strange, Koula, Radley. QLD — Ponga, Cobbo, Toia, Tabuai-Fidow, Fifita, Munster, Walker, Flegler, Grant, Tino, Nikora, Capewell, Cotter + Plath, Carrigan, Nanai, Horsburgh, Loiero, Walsh, Taulagi.
