The market has Bulldogs as home favourites at $1.73 H2H, but their five-game losing run is hard to ignore. Melbourne Storm are $2.11 and improving — Jahrome Hughes has been excellent in the halves and their forward pack is coming together. Taking Storm for the win at value odds.
