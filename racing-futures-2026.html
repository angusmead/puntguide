Short Answer

Australian racing futures markets cover the major Group 1 races including the Melbourne Cup, Cox Plate, Golden Rose and Epsom Handicap. Odds are available year-round from BetRight, Sportsbet and Ladbrokes. BetRight is the recommended bookmaker for racing futures due to Best Tote availability.

Key Findings
  • →BetRight is the recommended bookmaker for racing futures - Best Tote guaranteed and the deepest racing futures market in Australia
  • →Each-way betting is available on most Group 1 futures - typically 4 places at 1/4 odds for races of 8+ runners
  • →The Melbourne Cup is Australia's most wagered-on race and the first Tuesday in November is the biggest betting day of the year
  • →Field-only markets (excluding the favourite) offer value in large-field staying races where favourites are under-bet
