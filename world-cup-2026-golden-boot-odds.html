Golden Boot Analysis — World Cup 2026

Messi ($1.67) is the runaway Golden Boot favourite. He's scored consistently through the tournament and Argentina are still alive at $4.50 in the outright market - deep runs mean more chances at the top of the scoring charts. If Argentina make the final, Messi wins this.

Mbappe ($2.80) is remarkable value at second favourite despite France being out. His leaderboard tally was that dominant. The catch: he can't add to it. If any of the four remaining players (Messi in particular) go on a scoring run, Mbappe gets caught.

Kane ($9.50) and Bellingham ($15.00) are the English pair. Kane is the No.9 with a proven Golden Boot record (2018 winner). Bellingham has been getting goals from midfield. If England make the final, both are in the frame - Kane at more attractive value.

Oyarzabal ($126.00) is a longshot. He'd need multiple hat-tricks across the remaining games to catch the leaders. But at $126, he doesn't need to be favourite to be a fun each-way punt on Spain going all the way.
