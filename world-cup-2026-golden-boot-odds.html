Golden Boot Analysis — World Cup 2026

Mbappe $6.00 — The clear favourite, and rightfully so. 12 World Cup goals across 2018 and 2022 makes him the joint third-highest scorer in World Cup history among active players. France are co-tournament favourites, and Mbappe is their primary penalty taker and creative fulcrum. He won the Golden Boot in Qatar with 8 goals — including a hat-trick in the final. Hard to go past.

Kane $7.50 — The clinical finisher. Kane is the Premier League's all-time leading scorer and England's heartbeat. England face Croatia, Panama and Ghana in the group — Kane will score. If England reach the quarters or beyond, this is a serious contender. He tends to score when it matters, and the Golden Boot is exactly the kind of individual milestone that motivates him.

Haaland $15.00 — First World Cup. Norway face France, Iraq and Senegal in their group. If Norway progress — they will beat Iraq, and need a result against France or Senegal — Haaland becomes genuinely dangerous. His record of 64 goals in 74 games for Manchester City makes him arguably the most lethal finisher in world football right now. $15.00 is solid each-way value if you believe in Norway going deep.

Value Pick — Lamine Yamal $21.00 — 18 years old and already Spain's key creative player. He was the best player at Euro 2024 at 16. Spain are joint favourites to win the tournament. If Yamal plays every match and Spain go deep, his goal threat from the right — cutting inside onto his left foot — makes him excellent value at $21. A generational talent at his peak moment.

Messi and Ronaldo $17.00 — Emotional bets as much as statistical ones. Both are highly motivated for what are expected to be their final World Cup appearances. Argentina and Portugal are both capable of going deep into the knockout rounds. There is enough career motivation here — and enough quality around them — to make these legitimate selections rather than sentimental punts.

Our Pick

Mbappe WIN at $6.00. The form, the record, the role, the team — everything points to Mbappe. Each way cover: Kane at $7.50 (EW) if you want a second string. Speculative play: Lamine Yamal $21.00 each way for those chasing a longer-priced return.
