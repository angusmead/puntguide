Golden Boot Analysis — World Cup 2026

Mbappe $3.25 — The dominant favourite, and it is hard to make a case against him. 12 World Cup goals across 2018 and 2022 makes him the highest-scoring active player in World Cup history. France are the outright tournament favourites, and Mbappe is their primary penalty taker, captain and creative fulcrum. He won the Golden Boot in Qatar with 8 goals — including a hat-trick in the final. The form, the platform and the pedigree are all there. At $3.25, you are not getting rich on a WIN bet, but as the banker in an EW multi, he is almost obligatory.

Haaland $5.00 — This is the bet of the tournament. First World Cup for Haaland, and Norway face France, Iraq and Senegal in their group. Haaland will score against Iraq. If Norway progress — a realistic prospect — he becomes one of the most dangerous goalscoring threats in the competition. His record of 64 goals in 74 games for Manchester City is the benchmark for elite finishing right now. At $5.00 EW with 3 places at 1/4 odds, he pays $2.00 place if he finishes top 3 scorers. Outstanding value.

Kane $9.50 — The clinical finisher, slightly drifted from previous markets. Kane is England's all-time top scorer and thrives on individual milestone targets. If England progress through the knockout rounds, Kane will score. The $9.50 price represents fair each-way value — a solid second-string to Mbappe.

Lamine Yamal $31.00 — 18 years old and already Spain's key creative player. He was the best player at Euro 2024 at age 16. Spain are second favourites to win the tournament. If Yamal plays every match and Spain go deep — which is likely — his goal threat from the right makes him a live each-way selection at $31. A generational talent at exactly the right moment.

Messi ($18.00) and Ronaldo ($19.00) — Emotional and statistical cases stack up. Both are expected to be playing their final World Cups. Argentina and Portugal both have high tournament potential. There is enough squad quality around them — and enough personal motivation — to make these legitimate selections rather than pure sentiment.
