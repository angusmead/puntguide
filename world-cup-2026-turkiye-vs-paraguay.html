Match Preview & Analysis

Türkiye carry meaningful tournament momentum into the 2026 World Cup after their run to the Euro 2024 quarter-finals. Vincenzo Montella has built a settled tactical structure and Arda Guler — the Real Madrid teenager — provides creativity from advanced midfield positions. Kerem Aktürkoğlu’s wide play and Kenan Yildiz’s emerging Juventus form give the Crescent-Stars a genuine front three.

Paraguay arrive as the type of South American side that has frustrated bigger opponents at past tournaments — disciplined, physical, slow to break down. Antonio Sanabria leads the line and the squad has solid foundations through the middle. But the attacking quality required to seriously threaten a Turkish side that defends well in central areas is the missing piece.

The market splits Türkiye $2.05, Draw $3.30 and Paraguay $3.90. The Draw at $3.30 has some appeal given Paraguay’s reputation for tight margins, but $2.05 on the side with greater attacking quality is the value play. The Turkish midfield three with Guler, Calhanoglu and Kokcu should control the tempo.

Our read: Türkiye WIN $2.05. Tournament experience plus attacking quality should be the difference in a contest that may be tight at half-time but opens up in the second half.
